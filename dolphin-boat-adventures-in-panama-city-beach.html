What do you do when you swim with dolphins?

If you swim confidently, they may approach you to interact. Do not touch the dolphins. If they're wild, chances are that they're not used to being touched. So just swim confidently and quickly instead of floating around. Scuba gear can be used for this.

What is the best time of day to see dolphins in Panama City Beach?

In general, you can see dolphins whenever the sun is shining and the water is calm. Feeding is usually seen during an outgoing tide. You should choose a tour operator that operates on the water almost every day.
