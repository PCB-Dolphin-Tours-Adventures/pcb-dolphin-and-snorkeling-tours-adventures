How do you spot dolphins on the beach?

Watch for signs of whales or Dolphins. These include feeding birds, oily slicks, breaking wave crests, waves that are moving the wrong way, and big splashes.

You can swim with dolphins and interact with them in a way that is unlike other animal interaction programs. You can touch, hug, kiss, play, and even take them for a ride.

What is the difference between dolphin swim Adventure and dolphin Encounter?
