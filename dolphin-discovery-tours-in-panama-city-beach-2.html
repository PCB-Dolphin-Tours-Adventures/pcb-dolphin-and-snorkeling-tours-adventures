Do not touch the dolphins. Dolphins will initiate physical contact with humans if they want it. You will always lose all dolphins if you touch just one. This not only negatively affects dolphin behavior, but also irritates other people in your group because everyone loses out on the swim.

Do you get wet on dolphin tours?

Do dolphins need cold water?

As warm-blooded mammal, dolphins are more comfortable in tropical and temperate water environments.

Can you touch dolphins in the ocean?

Dolphins are curious creatures, but many of their behaviors can be misinterpreted as friendly when, in reality, they are signs of aggression or disturbance. Do not pursue or interact with a dolphin that approaches you on the water. Instead, take immediate action to get away.
